Materials We Buy

We purchase a wide range of ferrous and non-ferrous metals at competitive, market-reflective prices. Our state-certified scales ensure you get paid accurately for every pound.

Non-Ferrous Metals

Copper: Wire, tubing, radiators, and solids.

Aluminum: Cans, siding, wheels, extrusions, and turnings.

Brass & Bronze: Valves, fittings, and decorative items.

Stainless Steel: All grades, including sinks and industrial equipment.

Insulated Wire & E-Scrap: Various grades of insulated copper and aluminum wire.

Market Insight

Non-ferrous prices are highly sensitive to global market changes. We monitor trends daily to ensure you receive the best possible price.

Ferrous Metals

Prepared Steel & Iron: Short steel, plate & structural, cast iron.

Unprepared Steel: Oversized items requiring processing.

Vehicles & Auto Cast: Cars, trucks, and engine blocks (fluids must be drained).

Appliances: Refrigerators, washers, dryers, and stoves (some prep required).

Sheet Metal & Tin: Light gauge steel and tin products.

Market Insight

Ferrous metal prices are often influenced by domestic construction and manufacturing demand. We provide stable, competitive pricing.
